# Stakeholder & Vendor Orchestration — Reference Guide

**CV Competency:** Stakeholder & Vendor Orchestration
**Scope:** Cross-cutting — all stages
**Tracked In:** CONTRIBUTION-LOG.md (per programme)

---

## What This Competency Covers

The ability to identify, engage, and coordinate stakeholders across business, technology, and vendor organisations throughout the programme lifecycle, ensuring alignment, managing expectations, and resolving conflicts.

## Key Skills

- Stakeholder mapping and influence analysis
- Running effective Steering Committee presentations
- Managing vendor relationships (Temenos, SI partners, third-party)
- Facilitating cross-functional workshops and decision forums
- Escalation management and conflict resolution
- Communication planning across diverse audiences

## When to Apply

| Stage | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1 — Discovery | Stakeholder interviews, current-state workshops |
| 2 — Fit-Gap | Business stakeholder workshops, vendor capability reviews |
| 3 — Architecture | Architecture review board, vendor design reviews |
| 4 — Build | Vendor delivery coordination, change control |
| 5 — Testing | UAT coordination, vendor defect management |
| 6 — Migration | Vendor migration support, business data validation |
| 7 — Cutover | War room coordination, vendor on-site support |
| 8 — Hypercare | Vendor support transition, BAU handover |

## Tracking

This competency is cross-cutting — it does not own a specific artefact folder. Instead, contributions are logged in each programme's `CONTRIBUTION-LOG.md` under the relevant stage.

## Quality Signals

- Steering Committee meetings have clear agendas and action tracking
- Vendor SLAs are defined and monitored
- Stakeholder concerns are logged and resolved, not ignored
- Escalation paths are documented and tested
- Communication plans cover all audiences (not just technical)

## Steering Committee Value

Effective stakeholder orchestration ensures the programme stays aligned with business objectives, vendor risks are managed proactively, and decisions are made at the right level without bottlenecks.

## CV Narrative

> "I orchestrated X stakeholders across Y departments and Z vendors throughout the programme, maintaining Steering Committee alignment through regular checkpoints and resolving X escalations before they impacted delivery."
