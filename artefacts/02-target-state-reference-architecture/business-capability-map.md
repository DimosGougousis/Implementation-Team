## How to Use
1. List the bank's business capabilities from the discovery phase
2. Map each to the corresponding Temenos Transact packaged business capability
3. Flag coverage as Fit, Partial, or Gap
4. Partial and Gap items feed into the Fit-Gap Register (artefact 03)
