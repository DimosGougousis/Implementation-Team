# Communication Plan

Who gets told what, when during cutover.

## Stakeholder Groups

| Group | Members | Channel | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| War Room | Programme lead, tech leads, vendor | Teams/Slack channel | Real-time |
| Steering Committee | CTO, programme sponsors | Email + call | Key milestones |
| Business Users | Department heads, key users | Email | Pre/post cutover |
| IT Operations | Ops team, DBAs, network | Teams/Slack channel | Real-time |
| Vendor Support | Temenos support | Support portal + call | As needed |
| Customers | End customers | Website banner, SMS | Post go-live |

## Communication Schedule

| Time | Audience | Message | Channel | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-7 days | All stakeholders | Cutover date confirmed | Email | |

| T-1 day | War room | Final readiness check | Call | |

## Escalation Path

| Level | Trigger | Contact | Response Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Technical issue | Tech lead | 15 min |
| 2 | Unresolved after 30 min | Programme lead | 15 min |
| 3 | Rollback consideration | Steering Committee | 30 min |
| 4 | Rollback decision | CTO | Immediate |
