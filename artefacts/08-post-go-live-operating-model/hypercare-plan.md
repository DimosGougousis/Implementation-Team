# Hypercare Plan

Stabilisation → steady-state handover plan.

## Hypercare Phases

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Support Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| Intensive | Weeks 1–2 | Critical issue resolution, immediate fixes | Programme + Vendor on-site |
| Stabilisation | Weeks 3–4 | Issue trend reduction, process refinement | Programme + Vendor remote |
| Transition | Weeks 5–8 | Knowledge transfer, BAU team ramp-up | Joint programme/BAU |
| Steady State | Week 9+ | Full BAU ownership | BAU team |
