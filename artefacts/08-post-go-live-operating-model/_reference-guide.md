## What This Competency Covers

The ability to design sustainable post-go-live operating models that ensure the implemented Temenos Transact platform can be operated, supported, and evolved by the business long after the programme team disbands.

## Key Skills

- Designing target operating models with clear org structures and responsibilities
- Defining L1/L2/L3 support tiers with escalation paths and SLAs
- Establishing incident management processes with severity classification
- Designing change management processes for post-go-live changes
- Planning Temenos upgrade cadence and adoption strategies
- Managing hypercare phases with measurable exit criteria
- Building continuous improvement frameworks with feedback loops

## Quality Signals

- Operating model has named roles, not just team names
- Support tiers have clear scope boundaries and escalation criteria
- Incident management has severity definitions with response/resolution SLAs
- Change management defines standard, normal, and emergency paths
- Hypercare plan has measurable exit criteria (not just time-based)
- Continuous improvement has defined feedback sources and KPIs

## Steering Committee Value

A well-designed operating model ensures the bank can sustain the platform independently, reducing vendor dependency and long-term cost of ownership. Hypercare exit criteria provide objective evidence that the platform is stable.
