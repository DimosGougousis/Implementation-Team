## Improvement Process

1. **Capture** — Log improvement item with source and justification
2. **Prioritise** — CAB reviews and assigns priority
3. **Plan** — Add to change calendar or sprint backlog
4. **Implement** — Execute via change management process
5. **Verify** — Confirm improvement achieved
6. **Close** — Update backlog, measure impact
