## Quality Dimensions

| Dimension | Definition |
|---|---|
| Completeness | % of records with all mandatory fields populated |
| Accuracy | % of records matching source-of-truth |
| Consistency | % of records with no cross-field contradictions |
| Timeliness | % of records updated within the required window |
| Validity | % of records conforming to business rules |
